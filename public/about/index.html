About Us

Our laboratroy is apart of the Mycobacteria Research Labs at Colorado State University, Fort Collins CO. We are concerned with possible boosting strategies of the current vaccination provided for Mycobacterium tuberculosis, Bacillus Calmette–Guérin vaccine. As well as environmental effects on the host immune response to Mycobacterium tuberculosis infection due to envrionmental, or non-tuberculosis mycobacteria. Due to the complex nature of the bacillus we are constantly altering experimental approaches and methods. Dr. Marcela Henao-Tamayo is an expert in Flow Cytometric analysis of host cells for immune profiling. Along with our research scientist, Dr. Andres Obregon-Henao, and post-doctoral fellow, Dr. Taru Dutt, we are robust laboratory for flow cytometric analysis of murine models response to mycobacterial immune responses. Our PhD. student, Amy Fox, is currently developing an R-based data analysis pipeline for processing flow cytometric data associated with any type of cytometer.
